Main rules

In this game you are the commander of a warship. Your task is to survive as long as possible. At the top of gamescreen you can see current HP, your score and number of your shots. If you use a game controller - press the red button on it to shoot (if you hold it - nothing will happen).

In the game you have only 2 options:

  1. Moving in any direction
  2. Shooting

Any of your actions will be displayed on the game controller (move, shot, hit, etc.) There are also bonuses in the game that will improve your weapon or restore your HP. But be careful, if you collision an asteroid, the damage will reduce not only HP, but also weapon.

If you score 5,000 points, you have to fight the boss. We don't have any information about him, but try not to get close to him.
